I'm **Adib Abrar Kabeer** — I build production AI systems, health-tech platforms, and government-scale software from Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Currently a **Founding Engineer @ [Everstar](https://github.com/EverstarAI)** where I'm helping build **[Gordian](https://everstar.ai)** — the nuclear regulatory AI platform powering compliance automation for the US nuclear industry. Document intelligence, agentic OS, the whole stack. We raised a $4M pre-seed backed by Third Prime VC.

I also built **BinduHealth** product line at **[Bindulogic](https://github.com/BinduLogic)** as CTO — hospital management, clinical terminology (SNOMED CT / ICD / FHIR), e-prescription, LIS/RIS — currently live across 15+ Bangladesh government hospitals serving 2M+ patients, including Dhaka Medical College and the National Institute of Cancer Research.

When I'm not shipping software, I'm running **[Mantiq Studio](https://github.com/MantiqStudio)** — a 3D design and fabrication workshop where I build community hardware projects, custom prints, and open designs out of a Bambu Lab P1S, Snapmaker U1, and Ender 3 V3 SE.
